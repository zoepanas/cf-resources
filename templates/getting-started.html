Getting Started

We welcome you to the community fridge resource guide! See an overview of our mission, who we are, and some key resources to get started with.

Overview
Mission
We've compiled these resources by the community for the community to keep sustaining our community fridge network. We hope this living document will be a space for community fridges to learn how to establish relationships with food producers that have shared values and shared commitment to the community.
Who We Are
The Team
We're a group of students from New York City who recognized the difficulties that fridges were facing in different areas, with no centralized way to keep track of resources. Feel free to contact us at sj2472@columbia.edu if you want to connect!
